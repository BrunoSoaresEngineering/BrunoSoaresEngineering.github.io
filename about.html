About Me

Welcome! I am Bruno...

I am a professional with over 8 years of dedicated contribution to the field of sustainable power systems. Throughout my academic and career journey, I have personified behaviors such as persistence, hard work, dedication, and a can-do attitude. My passion for tackling exciting technical challenges has been a driving force in my professional career.

I hold a Master's Degree in Electrical and Computers Engineering from the Faculty of Engineering at Porto University (FE/UP). My educational background comprises a strong foundation of power systems, programming and automation. Combined with hands-on experience acquired on professional context, turns me into a versatile professional capable of delivering outstanding results.

During my Master Degree, I started to build a strong foundation in software development. This expertise was further solidified, over the past 5 years, by developing complex electrical simulation models using a range of programming languages, including C++, C, Python, and Fortran. Furthermore, I supported on several grid integration studies (running simulations, producing reports,...) and, in this context, I have managed a High Performance Computing simulation cluster in Azure Cloud for PSCAD simulations: cost control, scalability, access management, system desired state, etc.

On my current role, I am developing a Passenger Information System for a train operator, in a monorepo with microservices architecture, in a small team. This has been allowing me to acquire experience on Web Development using Node.JS and MariaDB for the backend and Svelte for the frontend. Cybersecurity and CI/CD pipelines for testing, linting, dependency management, building, SAST (Static Application Security Testing), etc. are also adopted in this project.

On previous positions I have worked with deep power system concepts like stability, control, protection systems, power generation, power electronics, renewables... I used many different simulation software that I now master, especially the dynamic simulation modules of: PSS/E, DigSILENT PowerFactory, PSCAD, TSAT, EMTP-RV...

If you are seeking a passionate and skilled professional who thrives on challenges and is committed to delivering excellence, I am eager to bring my expertise to your projects. Kindly have a further look to both my resume and this website pages where you can find much more information about my profile like portfolio, education, publications, skills, etc. Please don't hesitate reaching out to me if you have any question.

Let's collaborate to create something truly exceptional together!
