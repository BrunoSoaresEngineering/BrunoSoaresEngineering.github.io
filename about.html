About Me

Welcome! I am Bruno...

a seasoned professional with over 8 years of dedicated contribution to the field of sustainable power systems. Throughout my academic and career journey, I have consistently pursued excellence, personifying behaviors such as persistence, hard work, dedication, and a can-do attitude. My passion for tackling exciting technical challenges has been a driving force in my professional career.

I hold a Master's Degree in Electrical and Computers Engineering from the Faculty of Engineering at Porto University (FE/UP). My educational background comprises a strong foundation of power systems, programming and automation. Combined with hands-on experience acquired on professional context, turns me into a versatile professional capable of delivering outstanding results.

During my Master Degree, I started to build a strong foundation in software development. This expertise was further solidified, over the past 5 years, by developing complex simulation models using a range of programming languages, including C++, C, Python, and Fortran.

Beyond my technical skills, what sets me apart is my commitment to quality. I am not only persistent and hardworking but also dedicated and kind. Striving for perfection is not just a goal but a mindset that I bring to every project.

If you are seeking a passionate and skilled professional who thrives on challenges and is committed to delivering excellence, I am eager to bring my expertise to your projects. Let's collaborate to create something truly exceptional together!
